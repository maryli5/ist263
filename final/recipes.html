🥑 Avocado Toast with Egg

Ingredients: Bread, avocado, egg, salt, pepper

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash avocado with salt and pepper.
  3. Fry an egg sunny side up.
  4. Assemble and enjoy.

🍌 Overnight Oats

Ingredients: Oats, milk, chia seeds, honey, berries

  1. Combine ingredients in a jar.
  2. Mix and seal.
  3. Refrigerate overnight.
  4. Top with berries and serve.

🍓 Berry Smoothie

Ingredients: Yogurt, berries, almond milk, honey

  1. Add all ingredients to blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour and enjoy.

🍤 Garlic Shrimp & Veggies

Ingredients: Shrimp, garlic, broccoli, peppers, oil

  1. Chop veggies and roast at 400°F.
  2. Sauté garlic and shrimp in pan.
  3. Combine and serve.

🐟 Lemon Garlic Salmon

Ingredients: Salmon, lemon, garlic, butter, asparagus

  1. Place salmon on tray with asparagus.
  2. Top with lemon, garlic, butter.
  3. Bake at 400°F for 20 minutes.

🍲 One-Pot Chicken & Quinoa

Ingredients: Chicken, quinoa, broth, vegetables, spinach

  1. Sauté chicken and veggies.
  2. Add quinoa and broth.
  3. Simmer until cooked and stir in spinach.

🌱 Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients: Mixed veggies, soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic

  1. Prep and sauté veggies with garlic and oil.
  2. Add soy sauce and stir-fry until tender.

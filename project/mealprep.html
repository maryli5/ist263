📅 Step 1: Plan Your Meals in Advance

  • Write a weekly menu

🍲 Step 2: Batch Cooking for the Week

  • Roast batches of chicken, quinoa, and veggies
  • Make soups or chili to portion
  • Freeze grab-and-go meals

🛠 Step 3: Time-Saving Kitchen Hacks

  • Pre-cut and pre-wash produce
  • Use appliances like Instant Pot or Air Fryer
  • Cook with one-pot or one-pan recipes

🎯 Final Tip: Start small! Even prepping one meal can make a difference.

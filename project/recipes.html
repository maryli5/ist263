🥑 Avocado Toast with Egg

Ingredients: Bread, avocado, egg, salt, pepper

  1. Toast your bread until golden and crispy.
  2. Mash a ripe avocado with salt and pepper.
  3. Fry an egg sunny side up.
  4. Spread avocado on toast and top with the fried egg.

🍌 Overnight Oats

Ingredients: Rolled oats, milk, chia seeds, honey, berries

  1. Combine ingredients in a jar.
  2. Mix and seal the jar.
  3. Refrigerate overnight.
  4. Top with berries and serve.

🍓 Berry Smoothie

Ingredients: Yogurt, frozen berries, honey, almond milk

  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. Add to blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour and enjoy.

🍤 Garlic Shrimp & Veggies

Ingredients: Shrimp, garlic, bell peppers, broccoli, olive oil, salt

  1. Chop vegetables.
  2. Roast veggies at 400°F for 15 minutes.
  3. Sauté garlic in olive oil.
  4. Cook shrimp until pink.
  5. Serve with roasted veggies.

🐟 Lemon Garlic Salmon

Ingredients: Salmon, garlic, lemon, butter, olive oil, asparagus, salt, pepper

  1. Prep salmon and asparagus.
  2. Season and arrange on tray.
  3. Add garlic, butter, lemon slices.
  4. Bake for 15–20 minutes.
  5. Serve with garnish.

🍲 One-Pot Chicken & Quinoa

Ingredients: Chicken, quinoa, onion, bell pepper, garlic, broth, spinach

  1. Dice and season chicken.
  2. Add onion, garlic, and pepper.
  3. Add quinoa and broth.
  4. Simmer until done.
  5. Stir in spinach and serve.

🌱 Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients: Bell peppers, carrots, broccoli, snow peas, soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, ginger

  1. Prep all vegetables.
  2. Sauté garlic and ginger in sesame oil.
  3. Add veggies and stir-fry.
  4. Add soy sauce and toss.
  5. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
